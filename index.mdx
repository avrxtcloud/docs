## Quick Start Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get an API key">
    Request credentials from the AVRXT team and keep them in your server-side environment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate requests">
    Send your key in the `Authorization: Bearer <token>` header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use endpoint docs">
    Start from [/api/introduction](/api/introduction) and follow endpoint-specific payload specs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
